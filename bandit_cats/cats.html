Player Skills Algorithm
"Grumpy"
 Grumpy is greedy. He always chooses the slot machine that showed the highest reward rate on previous trials. That often results in sticking to the suboptimal choice and low overall reward.

"ε-greedy Algorithm"

for i in 1:n
    r = runif(0, 1)
    if r < ε:
        random action
    else:
        current-best action
"Pop"
 Pop doesn't care about previous results, just explores randomly. Since there is no exploitation at all, overall reward is always low.

"Random Choice"

for i in 1:n
    choice = runif(1, 3)
"Toast"
 Toast is smart. He uses previous information about the rewards to update his beliefs while still exploring. Mostly in all cases chooses the machine with the highest probability of reward.
