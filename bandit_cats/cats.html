Player Skills Algorithm
"Grumpy"
 Grumpy is greedy. In (1-ε)% of cases he chooses the slot machine that showed the highest reward rate on previous trials. That often results in sticking to the suboptimal choice and low overall reward.

Exploration: ε%
Exploitation: (1-ε)%

"ε-greedy"

for i in 1:n
  r = runif(0, 1)
  if r < ε:
    choice = runif(1, 3)
  else:
    choice = argmax(reward1,

"Pop"
 Pop doesn't care about previous results, just explores randomly. Since there is no exploitation at all, overall reward is always low.

Exploration: 100%
Exploitation: 0%

"Random Choice"

for i in 1:n
  choice = runif(1, 3)
"Toast"
 Toast is smart. After each action he updates his beliefs and before making a new action and does a random sampling based on that information. Mostly in all cases sticks to the machine with the highest probability of reward.

Exploration: ≈50%
Exploitation: ≈50%
